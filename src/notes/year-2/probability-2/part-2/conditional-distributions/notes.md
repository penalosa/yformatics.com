## Conditional mass function

$$\Bbb{P}(X = x | Y = y) = \dfrac{\Bbb{P}(X = x, Y = y)}{\Bbb{P}(Y = y)}$$

$$\Bbb{P}(X = x, Y = y)  = \Bbb{P}(X = x | Y = y) \Bbb{P}(Y = y)$$
